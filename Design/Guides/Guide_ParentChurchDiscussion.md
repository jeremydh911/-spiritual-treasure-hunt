Parent & Church Leader Discussion Guide (Mature Mode)

Purpose
- Provide leaders and parents with practical scripts, boundaries, and referral guidance for sensitive Mature‑Mode topics.

Contents
- Short scripts for initiating conversations (age 18+)
- Boundaries and consent checklists for continuing the conversation
- Referral flow: when to involve a pastor, counselor, or medical professional
- Suggested small‑group activities and leader prompts

Sample script (sexuality)
- "I want to talk about how God designed relationships and how we can keep each other safe. If you have questions, I'm here and we'll find someone who can help."

Confidentiality & reporting
- Explain what the church team will keep confidential and mandatory reporting rules (if applicable).

Downloadable resources
- One‑page printable guides for small‑group leaders
- PDF handouts for parents (available in the app under Mature Mode)

Pastoral resources
- Encourage churches to provide local counseling contacts and vetted reading lists.

Implementation note
- All Mature content in the app includes a 'Talk with your parent/pastor' CTA linking to these guides and to local church admin contact features.
