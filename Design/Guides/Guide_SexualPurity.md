Discussion Guide — Sexual Purity & Marriage (Mature Mode)

Purpose
- Provide parents and church leaders with conversation guides that affirm sexual goodness within marriage while teaching boundaries, consent, and Christian ethics.

Core messages
- Sex is a good gift from God designed for marriage; teach this positively and biblically.
- Emphasize respect, consent, bodily integrity, and healthy boundaries for teens.

Age‑appropriate conversation starters
- "What does the Bible say about love and marriage?"
- "Why are boundaries important in friendships and dating?"

Pastoral guidance for parents
- Use clear, non‑graphic language. Keep the focus on relationship health, mutual respect, and faithfulness.
- Provide resources for follow‑up (local pastoral counselors, trusted books).

Group discussion prompts
- What does healthy dating look like in our community?
- How can we help friends who feel pressured or confused about relationships?

Resources & referrals
- Provide a list of age‑appropriate books and church counseling contacts inside the app.
- Include a consent checklist and suggested parental follow‑up activities.
