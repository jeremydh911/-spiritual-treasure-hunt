Mature Mode — Topics & Content Outline

Purpose
- Provide age‑appropriate, theologically sound explanations for mature topics behind age verification and church override.
- Emphasize biblical faithfulness, pastoral care, and discussion guidance for parents and church leaders.

Access & gating
- Mature Mode is available for ages 18+ via age verification and is subject to church overrides.
- All Mature content is hidden for child profiles until consent is verified.

Topics included (Phase‑1 Mature Mode)
1. Nephilim / Ancient Giants
   - Scope: historical background, textual overview (Genesis 6; extra‑biblical mentions), theological cautions.
   - Purpose: understand Scripture references without sensationalizing; connect to God’s sovereignty and human responsibility.
   - Teaching hooks: why ancient texts mention them, how Christians read difficult passages, pastoral framing for fear/anxiety.

2. Sexual Purity & Marriage
   - Scope: positive teaching on sexuality as a gift from God, boundaries for minors, biblical marriage theology, pastoral counsel on purity.
   - Emphasis: sex is good and created for marriage; teach consent, respect, and bodily integrity in age‑appropriate language.
   - Resources: conversation starters, parental scripts, recommended follow‑up pastoral resources.

3. Kingdom Responsibility & Privileges
   - Scope: what it means to bear responsibility in God’s kingdom (service, stewardship, leadership), privileges of spiritual citizenship.
   - Practical: leadership ethics, servant leadership, carrying influence without pride.

4. Modern Idolatry (what idols are and how they affect life)
   - Scope: identifying contemporary idols (materialism, success, tech, status), spiritual effects, steps to repent and reorient.
   - Case studies: social media as an idol, career ambition, consumerism; reflection questions for kids/teens.

5. Prophecy & Revelation
   - Scope: biblical prophecy, how to read prophetic texts in context, discernment of contemporary prophetic claims, and pastoral safeguards.
   - Purpose: teach hermeneutical caution, how prophecy points to Christ, and practices for discerning and testing prophetic words within the church.
   - Teaching hooks: historical context, canonical criteria, pastoral reflection prompts, and case studies illustrating responsible interpretation.

Structure of each Mature topic
- Short exposition (age‑appropriate summary)
- Deeper theological note (Mature Mode only)
- Historical/contextual footnotes and Scripture references
- Pastoral discussion guide for parents/church leaders
- Suggested follow‑up readings and prayers

Safety & language policy
- No explicit sexual content; sexual ethics are taught responsibly and non‑graphically.
- Avoid sensational or conspiratorial framing of Nephilim; focus on Scripture and pastoral care.
- Always point back to Christ, repentance, and grace.

Acceptance criteria
- Each Mature topic includes ScriptureRefs and a theologicalNote.
- Parental consent gating works and church admin can disable Mature Mode for linked accounts.
- Printable discussion guides available for parents and church leaders.
