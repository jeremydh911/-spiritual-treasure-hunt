# Kingdom Responsibility & Privileges — Parent / Church Guide

**Focus:** servant leadership, stewardship, and spiritual citizenship. **Stewardship is emphasized as the foundational responsibility of kingdom citizens — include practical stewardship pathways in leader activities and youth programs.**

**Leader activities:** mentoring, service pathways, ethical leadership examples.

**Discussion prompts**
- How do we serve without seeking status?
- What privileges come with spiritual maturity?
