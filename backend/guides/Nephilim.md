# Nephilim — Parent / Church Guide

**Purpose:** offer a calm, Scripture‑based explanation for passages that mention the Nephilim (Genesis 6) and provide discussion prompts.

**Key points**
- Read Genesis 6 in context; avoid sensational interpretations.
- Focus on God's sovereignty and human responsibility.

**Discussion prompts**
- What does this passage teach us about sin and God's plans?
- How should we handle mysterious or difficult Bible passages?

**Resources:** recommended commentaries and pastoral contacts.
